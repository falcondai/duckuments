## Definition of time series

\begin{definition}  \label{def:time-series-def}

A \emph{time series} with domain $\aset{X}$ and time domain $\Time$ is a sequence of tuples
$\tup{t_i, x_i} \in \Time \times \aset{X}$.

\end{definition}
